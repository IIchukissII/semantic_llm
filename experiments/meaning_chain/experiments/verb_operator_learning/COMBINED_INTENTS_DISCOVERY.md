# Discovery: Combined Opposite Intents Create Richer Meaning

**Date**: 2025-12-27

## The Surprise Finding

**Hypothesis**: Pure cluster intents (all +love or all -love) should have higher coherence.

**Result**: **OPPOSITE** - Mixed/orthogonal intents have HIGHER coherence!

| Intent Type | Avg Coherence | Lasing Rate |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| Pure cluster | 0.467 | 50% |
| Mixed/opposite | **0.658** | **67%** |

## Why This Matters

Opposite intents create **semantic tension** that produces richer, more coherent meaning:

1. **love + learn** (+love vs -love)
   - Intent direction: -love (knowledge-seeking dominates)
   - But themes include: +sacred, -sacred, +beauty, +good
   - The **tension** between connection and observation creates nuance

2. **create + destroy** (orthogonal)
   - Intent direction: +good
   - Coherence: **0.79** (highest!)
   - The **dance of opposites** produces creative tension

3. **help + harm** (opposite Δg)
   - Intent direction: +life
   - Coherence: 0.78
   - Moral complexity creates depth

## Implications for Intent Design

1. **Don't fear contradiction**: Mixed intents are MORE coherent, not less
2. **Embrace paradox**: "love while letting go" produces better responses than pure love
3. **Use orthogonal pairs**: create+destroy, find+lose, begin+end enhance meaning
4. **Avoid pure clusters**: Single-direction intents may be too narrow
